About

Built for
Builders.

Resonance is the flagship technical festival of Electronics and Tele-communications department of Goa college of Engineering, bringing together the sharpest engineering and technology minds for three days of high-intensity competition, knowledge exchange, and collaborative problem-solving.

From 12-hour hackathons to precision robotics challenges, Resonance is the arena where ideas are stress-tested and talent is recognised.
